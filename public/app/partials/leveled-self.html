Leveled Self

A VR supply chain for receiving doses of inspirations and perspectives

Introduction

When we decided to curate VR experiences that audiences of different kinds wouldn’t have in other ways, we made some clear decisions about the content we create and include.

  • Living things had to disappear to create an environment for solitary thinking.
  • Both light and scale would play an important role in enabling different types of feelings.
  • Skewing the gravity in this world is one way to blend reality and virtual reality together.


Approach

Moving from abstraction to a familiar environment by engaging the act of “level design” in game design terms allowed us to create architectural spaces for interaction.

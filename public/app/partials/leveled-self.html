Introduction

When we decided to use VR to simulate a drug experience, we made some clear decisions about the content we create and include.

  • Living things had to disappear to create an environment for solitary thinking.
  • Light and scale would play an important role in distorting reality.
  • Skewing gravity could increase the unfamiliarity of a world.


Approach

By moving from an abstract environment to a familiar environment through engaging the act of “level design” terms allowed us to alter architectural forms for interaction.

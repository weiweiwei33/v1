Information

Hello, I'm Weiwei. I design and facilitate experiences.

Born in California, raised in Shanghai, I was originally from Taiwan, and is currently studying Interaction Design at California College of the Arts in San Francisco, California.

Previously, as the first designer to join co-working space and accelerator XNode in China, I focused on internal and external communication of company’s vision through delivering weekly design digests to the team, producing creative promotional materials and writing articles for their publication.

I am also an alumnus and founding member of John Bielenberg’s experimental education program Think Wrong Institute (formerly Project M), where we explore pathways to inspire and support individuals through the process of Thinking Wrong by working with progressive schools in San Francisco and Taiwan.

I learn, work, and play to decipher what it means to coexist with the environment she is in. I also work in the medium that’s most appropriate for the goal and apply fundamental theories on communication to a host of challenging and important entangled messes - such as climate change and education system - without fear, favor, or jargon.

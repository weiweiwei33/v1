Cyborg

Confronting the unknown future with ethics

Introduction

Very often, incremental progresses are made in ways that we neglect the power of accumulation. This project explored and challenged the my comfortability with the notion of direct manipulation of living creatures, specifically humans.

Focus

There are two briefs: Allowing anyone to erase the last 1 - 5 ideas they’ve had with a portable device, and embedding any existing sensor under someone’s skin. I chose to embrace one and push back on the other in order to undergo the thinking process when stretching ethical meanings.

Approach

To lend credibility to a breakthrough technology, the appearance of the product plays an important role. / To communicate the great uncertainties in this area, a video sketch can express our concerns adequently.

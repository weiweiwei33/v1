Button Up

An attempt to imagine a massive multiplayer offline game.

Introduction

It is very easy to turn toward the devices we carry at many different kinds of social environments. Having an alibi to initiate a conversation can often make the process simple.

Focus

Understand effective ways to encourage simple social interactions. Prototype a proof of concept with accessible materials and tools such as laser cutting and photon.

Approach

see documentation

First thing first

Content synthesis from interviews with two Industrial Design then-juniors. With very specific habits of avoiding social interactions, we wanted to increase the possibility of them interacting with classmates in school.

Narrowing the idea

We decided to make a simple game that is internet connected. Each player can invite anyone to play a 30 second button mashing game, and if the winner is the owner of the game, a text message would be sent to his or her partner informing the winning and encouraging the partner not to even try to win.

Introduction

Launching the first class in summer 2015, Think Wrong Institute is an immersive program allowing all participants from different to work on real world challenges in partnership with clients, organizations, and companies in the Bay Area.


Focus

Imagine a society where there are global mispriorities especially on the over-emphasis to the achievement of social status and the lack of attention to climate change. Most actions taken by participants of the society do not consider the harmony between themselves and the environment. This is not hypothetical, it was and it continues to be our biggest challenge of all time.


Approach

The effect of these mispriorities are massive in scale. We identified actionable streams to work on:

  • Give talks and run intensive workshops that support people to break away from status-quo thinking.

  • Identifying environemntal challenges that are typically masked in the noise of status-quo and creating actionable ways to tackle those problems.

  • Assisting our partners with re-framing their goals to align with environmental and social problems.

    Going off the Grid

    With support from the Stone Edge Microgrid Project in Sonoma, we are assisting TouCheng Leisure Farm in going off the grid. In Winter 2016, we installed The Energy Detective on all of the junction boxes on their property and will monitor their usage of electricity for twelve months. Afterwards we would provide them with proposals of energy generation and storage solutions, which will allow them to take their propoerty off of the traditional grid system.




    Bamboo Bikes

    At TouCheng Leisure Farm, there is a healthy amount of bamboo growing. After several discussions and seeing our past works, they invited us to set up a bamboo bike shop for them in two 20 feet shipping containers.

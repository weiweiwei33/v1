Thinking Wrong

The secret project to fuel meaningful actions

Introduction

Launched the first class in summer 2015, Secret Project is an immersive class allowing all students from different cultural and skill background to work on real world challenges in partnership with clients, organizations, and companies in the Bay Area.

Focus

Imagine a society where there are global mispriorities especially on the over-emphasis to the achievement of social status and the lack of attention to climate change. Most actions taken by participants of the society do not consider the harmony between themselves and the environment. This is not hypothetical, it was and it continues to be our biggest challenge of all time.

Approach

The complication of these mispriorities are massive in scale. We decided to identify actionable streams to work on:

  • Give talks and run intensive workshops.

  • Becoming more conscious of problems that have been masked in noises lowers the possibility to tackle entangled messes collectively. Ideate and execute environment related projects.

  • The effectiveness of talking always need to be amplified by relevant and meaningful actions. Modulate and scale.

  • Aligning priorities at a size that ispertinent is essential in confronting wicked problems.

    Going off the Grid

    With support from the StoneEdge MicroGrid Project in Sonoma, we are assisting TouCheng Leisure Farm in going off the grid. In Winter 2016, we installed The Energy Detective on all of the junction boxes on their property and will monitor their usage of electricity for twelve months.




    Bamboo Bikes

    At TouCheng Leisure Farm, there is a healthy amount of bamboo growing. After several discussions and seeing our past works, they invited us to set up a bamboo bike shop for them in two 20 feet shipping containers.
